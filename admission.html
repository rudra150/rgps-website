Admission Process

We welcome students from Grade 1 to Grade 12. Our admission process is designed to be transparent, simple, and inclusive. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis depending on seat availability.

  • Step 1: Fill out the admission form online or at our campus.
  • Step 2: Appear for the entrance test (for Grades 6 and above).
  • Step 3: Attend a parent-student interview with the principal.
  • Step 4: Submit required documents and complete the fee payment.
