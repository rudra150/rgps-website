About Rudra Goswami Public School

Rudra Goswami Public School (RGPS) was founded with a vision to empower young minds through quality education. We are committed to fostering a learning environment that nurtures curiosity, critical thinking, and compassion. Our faculty comprises highly qualified and passionate educators who strive to provide personalized attention to each student.

With modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, and technologically equipped classrooms, RGPS ensures a comprehensive academic experience. Our curriculum is designed to blend academic rigor with extracurricular activities that promote creativity and leadership skills among students.

We believe in the holistic development of children, offering a wide range of programs in arts, sports, science, and culture. Our values emphasize discipline, integrity, and inclusiveness. At RGPS, we prepare students not only for academic success but also to be responsible global citizens.

From kindergarten to senior secondary, RGPS instills a lifelong love of learning and a commitment to excellence. Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields and contribute meaningfully to society.
